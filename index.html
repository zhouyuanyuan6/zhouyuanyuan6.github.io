About Me

I am a fourth-year PhD student in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park advised by Professor Jin-Oh Hahn. Previously, I completed my BS in Energy and Environment System Engineering at Zhejiang University and my MS in Mechanical Engineering at Tennessee State University.

My research interests span multimodal physiological signal analysis, state-space modeling, closed-loop control, and machine learning, with hands-on experience in 3D modeling and manufacturing.
